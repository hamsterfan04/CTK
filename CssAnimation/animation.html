Sunset

A sunset as a noun is defined by Merriam-Webster as "the apparent descent of the sun below the horizon", but is also associated with endingd or decline, given the contect of it marking the end of a day. The opposite of a sunset is sunrise.
In literature and artworks, sunsets are typically symbolic of an ending-- think of the cowboys riding off into the sunset at the end of a film.
Additionally, sunsets are considered to be a quite romantic time of day, despite their associations with an ending.
