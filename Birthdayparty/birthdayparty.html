About the song

My Boo

Usher & Alicia Keys

Originally released August 29, 2004, "My Boo" maintained number one on billboard charts for 11 weeks, including the week of November 15th.

This song was written by the performers, as well as Jermaine Dupri, Adonis Shropshire, and Manuel Seal.

During the 47th Grammy Awards, "My Boo" won "Best R&B Duo Perfomance".
